How it works

Three steps.
Zero paper.

01

Pay as usual

Nothing changes at checkout. DigiSlips works silently in the background. No new hardware for the cashier, no extra steps for you.

02

Scan or tap

Tap your NFC card or scan the QR code on the DigiSlips device. Your slip is ready in seconds.

03

Your receipt, instantly

Share it, save it as PDF, or download the app to keep all your receipts permanently in one place.

For merchants

Plug in.
Go paperless.

DigiSlips installs between your existing POS machine and thermal printer. No software changes, no training, no disruption. Your till keeps working exactly as it does today.
