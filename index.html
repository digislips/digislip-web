How it works

Three steps.
Zero paper.

01

Pay as usual

Nothing changes at checkout. DigiSlip works silently in the background — no new hardware for the cashier, no extra steps for you.

02

Scan or tap

A QR code appears on the till screen. Scan it with your phone camera, or tap your NFC card if you've registered one.

03

Your receipt, instantly

Your full receipt opens in your browser — no app needed to view it. Available for 24 hours, with the app coming soon for permanent history.

For merchants

Plug in.
Go paperless.

DigiSlip installs between your existing POS machine and thermal printer. No software changes, no training, no disruption. Your till keeps working exactly as it does today.
